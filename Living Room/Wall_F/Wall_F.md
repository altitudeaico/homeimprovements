## 4. Design intent
Deep olive-green media wall, TV centred, **linear electric fire** below it, two **mirrored alcoves** (oak shelves over push-to-open cabinets), **brass sconces** either side of the TV, integrated 3000K LED. KEEP the chimney untouched; the fire sits in a new opening at the bottom.

**Two variants — owner to choose one:**
- **Variant A — modern:** oak **slatted / fluted** vertical panels flanking the TV; clean modern detailing. *(master_elevation_A_modern_slatted)*
- **Variant B — traditional:** **shaker** push-to-open doors, simpler panelling; traditional detailing. *(master_elevation_B_traditional_shaker)*

**Materials & finishes:** cabinetry & wall — Studio Green No.93 · shelves & mantel — natural oak (matt) · backs/strips — oak slatted (A) or shaker doors (B) · plinth/skirting — painted Studio Green · handles — brushed brass/gold · fire — electric linear (e.g. Evonic e1800).
**Lighting:** LED 3000K under shelves, under mantel, under cabinets/plinth, and behind/above the TV recess; wall sconces either side of TV.

## 9. Build sequence
1. `manuals/step3_build_new_frame.png` — 47×70 CLS frame in front of the chimney; **chimney opening kept clear at the bottom**; jack studs + noggins for TV/shelf fixings; ≥50–100mm clearance above the opening.
2. `manuals/step4_board_and_finish.png` — board (fire-rated around the opening), tape, fill, skim, sand.
3. `manuals/step5_cut_new_opening.png` — cut the fire aperture in the frame only (fire size + clearance); strengthen edges if >800mm.
4. `manuals/step6_install_fire_and_finish.png` — fit fire, route/hide cables, caulk, prime, paint, final touches.
